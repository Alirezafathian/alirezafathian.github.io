Classification of Alzheimer’s disease, mild cognitive impairment, and cognitively normal patients based on the rs-fMRI data


In this study, we are exploring the effects of Alzheimer’s disease (AD), early mild cognitive impairment (EMCI), and late mild cognitive impairment (LMCI) on functional brain connectivity. We are developing a machine learning model that enables us to classify patients with these disorders. Our goal is to develop a reliable method for diagnosing Alzheimer's disease based on fMRI data.

Data
The rs-fMRI and T1w images of 96 subjects (24 subjects for each group of AD, EMCI, LMCI, and CN ) from ADNI database are analyzing in this study.

Methods
We are developing a general tool to perform classification and also network analysis on any given dataset including rs-fMRI and T1w images of subjects that we want to classify. The general pipeline of this study is shown in the figure below.
