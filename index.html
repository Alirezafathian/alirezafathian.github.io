I am a master's student in mathematical biology at Tarbiat Modares University, where I study complex systems and computational neuroscience with Prof. Yousef Jamali.

My thesis investigates the changes in functional brain connectivity of Alzheimer’s Disease (AD) and Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI). We are developing a machine learning model that enables us to classify patients with AD, patients with MCI, and cognitively normal patients. Our goal is to develop a reliable method for diagnosing AD based on fMRI data.

Key Interests:
Complex Systems, Neuroimaging Data Analysis, Connectomics, fMRI Data Analysis, Mathematical Modeling, Machine Learning, Deep Learning.
